Descripción

Historic mansion house set in private parkland 7 miles from nearest train station in market town of Inverurie.

Secluded in beautiful parkland and surrounded by mature trees, Warthill is a seat of Clan Leslie. Full of history, but also warm and comfortable.

We offer 3 bedrooms with bathroom TV and tea/coffee making facilities. Cosy sitting room with open log fire plus dining room for breakfast and candlelit dinners if required. Broadband available.

Tour of the house available on request and its context in Scottish history. Happy to recommend good restaurants, specialised shopping etc

North East of Scotland is full of historical monuments, NT castles, interesting ruins, standing stones, all within a stone's throw

Train and bus stations in Inverurie...7 miles
Aberdeen Airport...20 miles

Whisky trail, wonderful beaches, local landmark Bennachie, granite city of Aberdeen. Excellent shopping in major university city. Several championship golf courses nearby.

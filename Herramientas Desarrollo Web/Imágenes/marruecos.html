Descripción

Located in a small village 15 minutes of Essaouira, Riad Titima is a heaven of peace, tranquility and relaxation.

(RIAD TITIMA EST UN HOTEL)
Located in a small Douar 15 minutes of Essaouira, Riad Titima is a haven of peace, tranquility and relaxation. The stone-built house stands in the middle of a park, with secular trees such as "Arganiers" and "Olives". The pool and terraces invite to relaxation and barbecues. Everything has been thought to offer you a dream vacation in the countryside, a stone's throw from the Atlantic Ocean.

Built in a neo-classical Moroccan style, typical of the area, the house offers all modern comforts, with a touch of traditionalism. The raw materials of the country are in the spotlight: stone, wood, brick, wrought iron, zelliges ... The whole forms a harmonious whole. Traditional Moroccan lounge is the center of the house, with its open fireplace to make a roaring fire when the evenings are cool. The rooms are built all around, with views of the pool or gardens. The upstairs bedrooms are complemented by a terrace with views of the surrounding countryside. Private bungalows offer families and couples the comfort and privacy in a corner of our area reserved for them. Each bungalow has a large bedroom, a spacious bathroom, a living room with fireplace with wood and a terrace.

Swimming pool, Terrace, Garden.
Loby, Dining room, fire place.
Free wifi access point.

European and Moroccan Managers.

Small typical village, country side, Ocean at 10KM, Essaouira city at 15Km.
Very quiet place.

Public bus system connecting the village to Essaouira downtown or the beach, Taxis, Shuttle in demand.
Airport and city Shuttle in demand.
Marrakech or Agadir Shuttle in demand.
